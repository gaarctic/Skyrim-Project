History

Skyrim is the northernmost province of Tamriel, known for its harsh landscapes, deep-rooted traditions, and fierce warriors. Its history is shaped by centuries of conflict, from the early Atmoran settlers to the rise and fall of the Dragon Cult, the reign of the Septim Empire, and the ongoing Civil War between the Imperial Legion and the Stormcloaks. Skyrim has always been a battleground for power, faith, and survival.

The province is home to the Nords, a proud and resilient people who value strength and honor. It has seen legendary figures like Ysgramor, the founder of the Companions, and Tiber Septim, the first Emperor of Tamriel. Today, Skyrim faces turmoil with the return of the dragons and the emergence of the Last Dragonborn, who must restore balance and confront Alduin, the World-Eater.

Main Quests

As the Dragonborn, you embark on a journey that will shape the fate of Skyrim. Some of the most significant quests include:

  • Dragon Rising: Discover your destiny as the Dragonborn after slaying a dragon and absorbing its soul.
  • The Way of the Voice: Train with the Greybeards and unlock the true power of the Thu'um.
  • Alduin’s Bane: Uncover an ancient prophecy and use the Elder Scroll to battle Alduin in Sovngarde.
  • Season Unending: Mediate peace between the Imperials and Stormcloaks—or choose a side in the Civil War.

Important Characters

Skyrim is filled with legendary figures, rulers, warriors, and mystics who play crucial roles in the story:

  • Ulfric Stormcloak: Leader of the Stormcloaks, he fights for Skyrim’s independence from the Empire.
  • General Tullius: The Imperial commander seeking to maintain Skyrim within the Empire.
  • Esbern: A surviving member of the Blades, dedicated to stopping Alduin.
  • Paarthurnax: A wise dragon who teaches the Dragonborn about the balance of power.
  • Delphine: A former Blade determined to restore the order and fight the dragons.

Skills & Items

Skyrim offers a wide range of skills and legendary artifacts:

  • The Elder Scrolls: Powerful ancient relics that hold the secrets of time and fate.
  • Daedric Artifacts: Rewards from the Daedric Princes, each with unique and powerful abilities.
  • Shouts (Thu'um): Ancient dragon powers unlocked by absorbing dragon souls.
  • The Ebony Blade: A Daedric weapon that grows stronger with betrayal.
  • The Dragonbone Weapons: Some of the most powerful weapons in the game, crafted from dragon remains.

Curiosities

Skyrim is rich in lore, secrets, and hidden details:

  • Skyrim's capital, Solitude, is home to the Empire’s presence in the province.
  • The Dark Brotherhood and Thieves Guild offer alternative paths for those who prefer stealth and intrigue.
  • Blackreach, a vast underground world, hides ancient Dwemer secrets.
  • Each Hold in Skyrim has its own political struggles, traditions, and rulers.
